Scientific Projects

Below are two major scientific domain projects that I have worked on.

1. M.Sc. Project (2021-22)

During my 6-month MSc project at Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR), I worked in the target preparation lab, specializing in thin film synthesis and optimization. My work focused on preparing self-supporting chromium thin films, from substrate preparation (copper films) to thickness measurement using alpha particles. I also gained hands-on experience with electron gun setups for boron thin films, material pellet preparation, and chemical processes like etching. To consolidate my learnings, I created a detailed poster on chromium thin film synthesis, showcasing the technical and analytical skills I developed.
